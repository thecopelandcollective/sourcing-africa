The mission for Sourcing Africa is to introduce North and South American and European interior designers to communities who have little or no outside opportunities.

As hosts on the continent, offer scheduled meetings and presentations, an exclusive behind-the-scenes access, and thrilling immersion in destinations, terrain and lodges only in the most desirable neighborhoods.

Stimulating ideas off the path of predictable design, we offer international designers the opportunity to work side by side with the many African masters to develop creations that will be part of their interiors and finely pursued lineups that aren't ethnic but global in their aesthetic.

This is the spirit of the Sourcing Africa, a well-fitted bridge that connects interior designers and artisans who wish to create and offer unique pieces under the guise of the finest in Africa.
