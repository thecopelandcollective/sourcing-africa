The mission for Sourcing Africa is to introduce North and South American and European interior designers to communities who have little or no outside opportunities.

We, as hosts in our country, offer scheduled meetings and presentations, an exclusive behind-the-scenes access, and thrilling immersion in destinations, terrain and lodges only in the most desirable neighborhoods.

Stimulating ideas off the path of predictable design, we offer international designers the opportunity to work side by side with the many African masters to develop creations that will be part of their interiors and product lineups that aren't ethnic but global in their aesthetic.

This is the spirit of the Sourcing Africa, a well-fitted bridge that connects interior designers and artisans who wish to create and offer unique ranges under the guise of the finest in Africa.

A Unique Opportunity

South Africa is known for its creativity and flair when it comes to decor, design, artisans, and forgers.

At Sourcing Africa we source the top talent our region has to offer. Being on the ground in South Africa, we are able to discern which artists have products which work well on an international level, yet remain unique and export cost effective.

Our transformative, small-designer personal meetings encourage local fellow artists, designers, town and regional planners, architects, sculptures, ceramic and pottery makers, leather and mohair mills, galleries, showrooms, glass and porcelain blowers, and weavers and beaders in the South African community to collaborate and enter the global theatre.

This is the spirit of the Sourcing Africa, a well-fitted bridge that connects interior designers and artisans who wish to create and offer unique ranges under the guise of the finest in Africa.
