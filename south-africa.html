South Africa

A Unique Opportunity

South Africa is known for its creativity and flair when it comes to decor, design, artisans, and metal forgers.

At Sourcing Africa we source the top talent our region has to offer. Being on the ground in South Africa, the team can establish which artists have skills which work well on an international level, yet remain locally unique.

Our transformative, one-on-one consults encourage local fellow artists, designers, town and regional planners, architects, sculptors, ceramic and pottery makers, leather, fabric, and mohair mills, galleries, showrooms, glass and porcelain blowers, and weavers and beaders in the South African community to collaborate and enter the global theatre.

This is the spirit of the Sourcing Africa, a well-fitted bridge that connects global interior designers and artisans to local artisans.
